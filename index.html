About Us

Swastik Classes, previously VMC Anand Vihar-Ghaziabad, was started by Mr. Shobhit Gupta and Mr. Alok Bansal. Mr. Shobhit Gupta himself secured an AIR 15 in IIT-JEE and has done B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering from IIT Delhi. He has been in the field of education since 15 years, teaching Physics and Mathematics. He is an exemplary teacher who proves to be a source of motivation for all JEE aspirants, counselling them through the years of preparation. He is an expert when it comes to time & stress management and has mentored students at all levels to help them achieve their true potential.
